Camera becomes luminous signal. Microphone becomes motion. Stack two neon synth visuals, bend their texture, trail, glow, bitrate, and color drift, then record the live canvas with audio.

For iPhone/iPad, start from a tap so Safari can unlock camera, mic, and audio analysis. MP4 export appears when the browser supports MediaRecorder MP4; otherwise the app records a WebM fallback.
